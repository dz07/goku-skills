# Moroccan Dairy Competitors Analysis

## COPAG — lait.ma
- **Colors**: Navy `#191970`, Royal blue `#4169e1`, White
- **Fonts**: Garotte, DK Black Bamboo, LEMON MILK, Almarai
- **Layout**: Hero slider with product images, product grid, cooperative story
- **Vibe**: Cooperative pride, volume, trust in numbers
- **Strengths**: Strong product photography, slider-heavy hero
- **Weaknesses**: Dated design, heavy fonts, slow load

## Chergui — chergui.ma
- **Colors**: Blue `#005da9`, Teal `#1ea0c3`, Orange `#ff9900`, Light gray `#f1f2f2`
- **Fonts**: Montserrat, Lato, Open Sans, Dax
- **Layout**: Slick carousel hero, product section, clean footer
- **Vibe**: Modern, playful, sport-oriented (sport bottles prominent)
- **Strengths**: Clean modern design, good mobile experience
- **Weaknesses**: Generic feel, could be any brand

## Danone Morocco — danone.com/fr
- **Colors**: Dark blue `#3a4570`, Cyan `#0096db`, Amber `#ffc168`, White
- **Fonts**: System fonts + CSS variables
- **Layout**: 8+ content sections, heavy on storytelling
- **Vibe**: Corporate, global, health-focused
- **Strengths**: Rich content, strong CSR messaging, nutrition focus
- **Weaknesses**: Too corporate, loses Moroccan identity

## Centrale Danone
- **Colors**: Red + White (Centrale heritage)
- **Vibe**: Mass market, everyday dairy
- **Strengths**: Brand recognition, wide distribution
- **Weaknesses**: Dated web presence

## Differentiation Opportunities for Albane
1. **Premium positioning** via gold accents + elegant typography (vs mass-market competitors)
2. **3D product showcases** — none of the competitors use interactive 3D
3. **Moroccan identity** — competitors either too generic or too corporate
4. **Bilingual excellence** — most competitors handle AR/FR poorly
5. **Modern animations** — GSAP scroll-driven storytelling vs static pages
6. **Mobile-first** — outperform competitors on mobile UX
