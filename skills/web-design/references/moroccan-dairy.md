## Common Typography

- **Arabic headlines**: Amiri (serif), Noto Sans Arabic (modern)
- **Latin headlines**: Playfair Display, Montserrat
- **Body text**: Noto Sans Arabic, Open Sans, Lato
- **Display/branding**: DK Black Bamboo, LEMON MILK, Garotte (custom)

## Layout Conventions

- Hero sections: full-viewport with product imagery or video
- Product grids: 3-4 columns on desktop, single column mobile
- Color themes: blues + white dominate (trust, freshness, dairy association)
- Gold/warm accents for premium positioning
- RTL layout standard for Arabic-first sites

## Section Structure (typical dairy landing page)

1. Fixed navbar with logo + nav links + CTA
2. Hero with large headline, subtext, product image/3D
3. Brand story / about section (split layout)
4. Product showcase (card grid)
5. Stats / impact numbers
6. Testimonials or partnerships
7. CTA band
8. Footer with links, social, contact
